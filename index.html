Program Preview

A complete pathway with purpose

Students move through a structured sequence that develops computational thinking, programming confidence, and career-connected skills.

9th Grade

Computer Science 1

Students begin with foundational computing concepts, digital problem-solving, and the habits needed for success in modern technology courses.

10th Grade

AP Computer Science Principles

A broad introduction to computing, programming, data, the internet, and the impact of technology in the real world.

11th Grade

AP Computer Science A

An in-depth Java course focused on algorithms, object-oriented programming, logical design, and structured code development.

12th Grade

Independent Study

Students apply what they have learned through advanced projects, research, portfolio development, and college-level preparation.

The showcase is more than display. It is evidence of learning.

Student work in computer science should reflect process, persistence, and problem-solving. This page can grow over time into a strong public-facing representation of the program’s academic quality and student outcomes.

  • Project thumbnails

Why showcase student work?

Authentic work makes program quality visible

A strong student showcase helps communicate what learners can do, not just what courses they take. It gives students pride in their work and helps families, counselors, and partners see the strength of the pathway.

  • Supports student confidence and ownership
  • Highlights real-world technical growth
  • Strengthens program visibility
  • Builds college and career narrative value

Suggested future additions

  • Embedded project clips or demo videos
  • Senior capstone highlights

  • Quotes from students about what they learned
